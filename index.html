What can you do in Győr?

Xantus Janos ZOO

Xantus János Zoo is a very cool zoo in Győr, which is constantly developing and receives a lot of attention from the city's leadership. One of my dear friends is a sculptor, and many of the carved sculptures in this zoo are thanks to him.

Official Site

Sports and Dance

Győr is a world-renowned city in women's handball, and it excels in numerous other sports and dance as well. If you think of Győr, you think of handball and ballet.

More

Night Clubs and Pubs

What could be better than a weekend relaxation? The city offers numerous nightclubs, pubs, and restaurants. The experience is enhanced by the fact that the downtown area of Győr is one of Hungary's most beautiful Baroque-style cities. In addition to partying, the Baroque Festival can also be a great experience.
